# Cookie-Clicker
A Fun AI Generated Cookie Clicker

A fun incremental game inspired by the classic Cookie Clicker, built with custom features like **AFK Mode**, **Rainbow Cookie**, and more! Click cookies, unlock upgrades, and watch your cookie empire grow.

---

## 🎮 Features

- **Core Gameplay**
  - Click the cookie to earn points.
  - Buy upgrades to increase cookie production.
  - Unlock achievements as you progress.

- **Special Cookies**
  - **AFK Mode **: Generates cookies automatically while you’re away.
  - **Rainbow Cookie**: Rare spawn that gives a massive cookie boost.
  - **Golden Cookie**: Classic bonus cookie with random effects.
  - **Mystery Cookie**: Unlocks hidden surprises when clicked.

- **Upgrades & Power-ups**
  - Auto-clickers to boost passive income.
  - Multipliers for faster cookie growth.
  - Prestige system for long-term replayability.(Planned)

- **Game Modes**
  - **Normal Mode**: Standard cookie clicking fun.
  - **AFK Mode**: Idle-friendly gameplay with auto-generation.
  - **Challenge Mode**: Limited resources, test your strategy.(Planned)

- **Visuals & UI**
  - Colorful cookie animations.
  - Progress bars and stats tracking.(Planned)
  - Achievement pop-ups.(Planned)
